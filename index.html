Analytical Index (CFI) Explanation

The **Composite Fit Index (CFI)** calculates your unified percentile fit by looking at each configured board. If you excel on 1m but don't compete on Platform, the system dynamically omits platform from the weighted matrix so your springboards shine.

If you have platform scores, we highly recommend toggling them on! Even moderate platform scores can dramatically elevate your recruiting profile for division championship rosters.
